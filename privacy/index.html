Privacy Policy

Version 1.2 | Effective Date: June 9, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Conspiral. Conspiral is a social trivia and party game focused on theories, conspiracies, ideas, and public beliefs. Players estimate how many people they think believe in various conspiracy-related topics and compare their answers with others.

Important: Conspiral is intended as an entertainment product. The game presents prompts, theories, and discussion topics for social gameplay purposes. Conspiral does not present conspiracy theories as factual claims and does not verify, endorse, or represent the accuracy of any theory, belief, or idea discussed within the game.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect information when you use Conspiral, our website, and related services.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact admin@conspiral.co.uk.

Who We Are

Conspiral is a mobile social trivia and party game currently available for Android devices. Future versions may also be released for iOS and iPadOS.

Conspiral is intended for users aged 18 years and older and is not directed toward children.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

If you choose to sign in using Google Sign-In, we may receive your Google account email address, Google display name, and authentication information necessary to create and manage your account. Providing this information is optional because users may also play as guests.

When you create an account, we may store user account identifiers, authentication records, profile information, and entitlement or purchase ownership information.

Guest Play Information

Users may play portions of Conspiral without creating an account. Guest play may store information locally on your device, including app settings, selected decks, local game progress, and temporary gameplay information.

Guest data generally remains on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the application. Guest play is not intended to identify you unless you choose to sign in or use online features that require account information.

Gameplay And Multiplayer Data

When using online features, we may process information required to operate multiplayer sessions, including room IDs, join codes, display names, team names, session identifiers, deck selections, round state, guesses, scores, locks and gameplay actions, connection and presence information, session events, and synchronization data.

This information is used to operate multiplayer games, synchronize gameplay, reconnect players, detect invalid or abandoned sessions, prevent abuse and fraud, troubleshoot service issues, and complete game sessions.

Purchase Information

If you purchase paid content, including deck packs or other digital items, we may process product identifiers, transaction identifiers, purchase tokens, receipt information, purchase timestamps, verification records, entitlement records, and store-provided purchase responses.

Purchase information is used solely for purchase verification, ownership confirmation, restoring purchases, fraud prevention, customer support, and compliance with legal and app store requirements. Purchase information is not used for advertising purposes.

Approximate Location Information

Conspiral does not intentionally collect precise GPS location information. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob or Google services may process IP-derived approximate location information.

Approximate location may be used for analytics, advertising, fraud prevention, security, and legal compliance. This does not include precise GPS location tracking.

App Activity Information

We may collect information regarding how the application is used, including app interactions, feature usage, gameplay events, ad interactions, ad impressions, ad clicks, and session activity. This information helps us improve the game, measure performance, operate advertising services, detect abuse, and maintain platform security.

Device And Technical Information

We and our service providers may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, session identifiers, operating system information, application version information, diagnostic information, crash information, and performance data. This information helps us maintain and improve the service.

Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

Conspiral is not designed to collect:

  • Precise GPS location
  • Health or fitness information
  • Private messages
  • User photos or videos
  • Audio recordings
  • Documents or files
  • Calendar information
  • Contact lists
  • Web browsing history

Third-party services may still collect technical device information necessary for their operation.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate Conspiral
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Enable online multiplayer
  • Verify and restore purchases
  • Deliver paid content
  • Maintain game functionality
  • Analyze performance and improve user experience
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Meet legal obligations
  • Respond to support requests

Advertising

Conspiral uses Google AdMob to display advertising. Advertising may include banner advertisements and interstitial advertisements.

Google AdMob and related services may process advertising identifiers, approximate location information, device information, app interaction information, ad impressions, ad clicks, diagnostic information, and consent preferences.

Where available, Conspiral may use Google's consent management tools and consent flows. Users may be able to manage advertising consent through available in-app settings or through their device settings where supported. Advertisements are intended to appear during natural breaks in gameplay.

Purchases And Paid Content

Android purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. To verify ownership and prevent unauthorized access to paid content, Conspiral uses server-side verification systems.

Purchase records and entitlement information may be stored through our backend services to verify ownership, restore purchases, prevent fraud, protect paid content, and support customer service.

Paid deck content is protected by access controls and is intended to be available only to users who have purchased or otherwise been granted access to that content.

Third-Party Services

Conspiral currently uses services including:

Supabase

Used for authentication, user profiles, multiplayer functionality, database services, purchase verification records, entitlement management, account management, and account deletion processing.

Google Sign-In

Used for user authentication and account creation.

Google Play Billing

Used to process Android purchases.

Google AdMob

Used to display advertisements and manage advertising-related functionality.

Future iOS versions may use Apple services where necessary.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with trusted service providers when necessary to operate the service, including authentication providers, hosting providers, payment and billing providers, advertising providers, analytics providers, fraud prevention services, and legal or regulatory authorities when required.

Some information processed by advertising and analytics providers may be shared as part of those services' normal operation.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, including:

  • HTTPS and TLS encryption during transmission
  • Server-side purchase verification
  • Access controls
  • Restricted service credentials
  • Supabase Row-Level Security protections
  • Security monitoring and fraud prevention measures
  • Platform security protections where applicable

However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary. Generally:

  • Account information is retained while an account remains active.
  • Multiplayer and session information may be retained for service operation, troubleshooting, and abuse prevention.
  • Purchase and entitlement information may be retained for verification, refunds, fraud prevention, accounting, tax, legal, and app store compliance purposes.
  • Guest play information remains on the device until removed by the user.

Retention periods may vary depending on operational, legal, or regulatory requirements.

Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account in-app from Settings > Account > Delete Account, through the website at https://conspiral.co.uk/delete-account/, or by emailing admin@conspiral.co.uk.

Please send deletion requests from the Google email address associated with your Conspiral account using the subject line "Account deletion request". Following verification, we will delete information where technically and legally possible.

Deletion may include:

  • Authentication account records
  • User profile records
  • Roles and permissions
  • Active entitlements
  • Account access

Certain records may be retained where required or reasonably necessary, including:

  • Purchase receipts
  • Fraud prevention records
  • Audit records
  • Tax and accounting records
  • Legal compliance records
  • App store compliance records

Where possible, retained records may be detached from the deleted account.

To submit a privacy-related request, contact admin@conspiral.co.uk.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside your country of residence through our service providers. Where required, appropriate safeguards are implemented to protect personal information during international transfers.

Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When significant changes occur, we may update the effective date and provide additional notice where required.

Continued use of Conspiral after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

For privacy questions, support requests, or account deletion requests, please contact admin@conspiral.co.uk.
