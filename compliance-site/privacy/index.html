Privacy Policy

Version 1.1

This Privacy Policy explains how Conspiral collects, uses, shares, stores, and deletes information when you use the Conspiral mobile game and related services.

The privacy contact for Conspiral is Accounts@astra-nomical.com. Use this email for privacy questions, account access issues, purchase restore problems, content concerns, and account deletion help.

Guest Play And Account Data

Conspiral can be played as a guest. Guest play may store local settings, selected decks, local game progress, and temporary gameplay state on your device. Guest data is not designed to identify you unless you choose to sign in or join online features using information that identifies you.

If you sign in with Google, Conspiral uses Supabase Auth to process your account identifier, email address, display name, avatar URL, session tokens, profile record, and app roles. This is used for account access, admin access where applicable, paid deck ownership, restore purchases, and account deletion.

Gameplay, Multiplayer, Purchases, And Ads

Online multiplayer uses Supabase to store room IDs, join codes, player display names, team names, device/session identifiers, room status, deck choices, round state, guesses, locks, scores, events, and connection or presence information. This data is used to synchronize the game between players, reconnect players, detect invalid rooms, and complete game sessions.

Deck and gameplay data includes selected decks, prompt IDs, categories, estimates, answers, scoring, timer settings, and local preferences. Paid deck card content is protected by server-side access rules and is only intended to load when a user owns the relevant deck or has an admin role.

Android purchases use Google Play Billing. Conspiral may process store product IDs, purchase tokens, transaction IDs, verification status, raw store responses, purchase timestamps, and related entitlement records. Supabase stores purchase receipts and deck entitlements so purchases can be verified, restored, protected from fraud, and linked to your signed-in account.

Future iPhone or iPad versions may use Apple in-app purchases. If released, Conspiral may process Apple product IDs, transaction IDs, receipt or server notification data, verification status, and entitlement records for the same purchase verification, restore, fraud prevention, and support purposes.

Conspiral uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may process advertising identifiers, IP-derived approximate location, device information, app interaction data, ad impressions, ad clicks, diagnostic data, and consent signals depending on your region, device settings, and consent choices. The app uses the Google consent flow where available, and you can reopen it from Settings > Manage Ad Consent.

Ads may include banner ads and interstitial ads. Interstitial ads are intended to appear at natural breaks, such as before score reveal, and should be closed using the ad provider's own close control. Conspiral may later offer a paid ad-free upgrade through the relevant app store billing system.

Sharing And Use

Conspiral does not intend to sell personal information. Data may be shared with service providers that operate the app: Supabase for authentication, database, storage, edge functions, and session services; Google for Google Sign-In, Google Play Billing, Android services, and AdMob; and Apple for app distribution and purchases if an iOS version is released.

Data is used to provide the game, operate multiplayer rooms, protect paid content, verify purchases, restore purchases, show and manage ads, provide support, respond to privacy or deletion requests, improve reliability, prevent abuse or fraud, comply with app store rules, and meet legal or tax obligations.

Conspiral uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including Supabase row-level security, server-side purchase verification, restricted service keys, HTTPS transport, and Android settings that disable app backup and cleartext traffic. No system can guarantee absolute security.

Retention And Deletion

Account data is kept while your account remains active. Multiplayer session data and purchase records may be retained as needed to provide the service, troubleshoot issues, prevent fraud, process refunds, comply with tax/accounting/legal duties, or enforce app store requirements. Local guest data may remain on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app.

You can request account deletion in the app from Settings > Account > Delete Account. Deletion removes the Supabase Auth user, profile, roles, active entitlements, and associated account access where technically and legally possible. Some purchase receipts, audit records, or fraud-prevention records may be retained where required or reasonably necessary.

If you cannot access the app, email Accounts@astra-nomical.com from the email address linked to your Google sign-in account with the subject "Account deletion request". Include enough information to identify the account.

Rights, Children, And Updates

Conspiral is not directed to children. The app is intended for users old enough to use online multiplayer, advertising, and app store billing features in their region. Do not submit personal information if you are not permitted to use these features.

Privacy laws may give you rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain data processing. To make a request, contact Accounts@astra-nomical.com. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

This policy may be updated as Conspiral changes, including when paid ad removal, iPhone support, additional stores, new multiplayer features, or new providers are added. Material changes should be reflected in the app and, where required, in the store listing.
